# Hit Play
Find a spotify playlist curated to your current mood!


<h3>How It Works</h3>
Type your mood into the search space. Then, press tbe search button or your enter key and wait to find your new favourite playlist! 

<h3>About It</h3>
Currently the system recognises 84 different emotions! Additionaly, while a spotify link is embedded as the main feature of the page, links to apple music, amazon and youtube playlists are also included at the bottom for users that use other platforms to listen to music.

<h3>The Code</h3>
The system contains hard coded emotions that are recognised and connected with a certain page. It may not be the most ideal system but it mainly works with what I am doing and is pretty much the most I can do at the moment, but I am proud of it and the skills that I picked up whilst making it. 
It also features moving text on the home page and embedded playlists for each
